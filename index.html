FAQ

Questions, answered

The four things everyone asks on the demo call.

Do I keep my current number?

Yes. We forward your existing line to your AI receptionist. Your callers dial the same number they always have and won't notice any difference. Cancel, and your number works exactly as before.

Will it sound robotic?

No. It uses a warm, natural voice and greets callers with your business name and your tone. Most callers have a normal conversation without realizing they're talking to an AI, and it never gets flustered, tired, or rude.

What happens if a caller has an emergency?

You decide what counts as urgent, and the AI follows your rules: transferring those calls straight to your cell, or flagging them at the top of your summary so you can call back first.

Is there a contract?

No contract. Every plan starts with a one-time setup (from $750) to build and tune your receptionist, then a flat monthly rate (from $500/mo), month to month. If it's not paying for itself, cancel. You keep your number either way.
